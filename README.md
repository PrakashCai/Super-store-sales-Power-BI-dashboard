📊 Project Overview

This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of a multi-national retail corporation's performance.
The project transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights across three core dimensions: Executive Performance, 
Product Profitability, and Geographic Trends.

🎯 Key Business Questions Answered

Executive View: What are our primary KPIs for Sales, Profit, and Returns, and how are they trending monthly?

Product Performance: Which specific product categories are driving growth, and which "Loss Leaders" need immediate attention?

Regional Analysis: Where are our most profitable geographic hubs, and is there a correlation between sales volume and return rates?

🚀 Dashboard Pages & Features

1. Executive Overview

High-Level KPIs: Real-time tracking of Total Sales (₹1.57M), Profit (₹175K), and Quantity.
Trend Analysis: Monthly sales forecasting and performance tracking.
Payment Insights: Breakdown of sales by payment mode (COD, Online, Cards) to understand customer behavior.

2. Product Performance

Profitability Deep-Dive: Analysis of profit margins by category, identifying Technology as the leading sector.
Loss Analysis: Bar Chart used to pinpoint sub-categories that are detracting from the bottom line.
Inventory Insights: Identifying top 10 products by sales to optimize stock levels.

3. Regional Sales Analysis

Geographic Distribution: State and City-level performance ranking, highlighting California and NYC as primary hubs.
Performance Correlation: A Scatter Plot analysis comparing Total Sales vs. Total Profit by Region to identify market efficiency.
Interactive Filtering: Advanced slicers for Year, Category, and Region for granular data exploration.

🛠️ Technical Toolkit

Data Cleaning: Power Query (ETL) used for data transformation, handling nulls, and formatting currency/dates.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):

Measures created: Total Profit, Profit Margin %, Return Rate %, Total Quantity, Total Return, and Total Sales.

Columns created: Month name, Month number, Profit category, and Year.

UI/UX Design: Implemented a clean, consistent color palette with intuitive navigation buttons and button-style slicers for 
enhanced user experience.


📈 Impact & Recommendations

Based on the dashboard findings, the following business recommendations were identified:

Reduce Return Rates: Investigate the high return rate (9.89%) in the West region, specifically within the Appliances sub-category.

Optimize Product Mix: Phase out or re-price "Tables" and "Supplies" sub-categories as they are currently operating at a net loss.

Regional Expansion: Replicate the high-margin sales strategies used in the West region across the Central and South regions.
